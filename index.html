About Us

Who we are, what we do, and why it matters.

NEMO (Northwestern Emergency Medical Organization) is a student-run organization dedicated to promoting awareness of emergency medicine and building a campus culture of readiness. Our mission is to equip Northwestern students with the knowledge, confidence, and certifications needed to respond effectively in a medical emergency.

  • Hands-On Training: EMT-B licensing courses, CPR/AED, and First Aid workshops led by certified instructors.
  • Campus & Community Outreach: Safety education events and volunteer service across Northwestern and Evanston.
  • Leadership & Mentorship: Members gain experience in peer education, leadership, and teamwork in high-stakes environments.

Together, we aim to strengthen preparedness across campus and inspire future leaders in healthcare and emergency response.
